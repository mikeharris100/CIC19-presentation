IQRI
Image Quality Ranking Index

$$ IQRI_{t} = \frac{v}{w_{t} + \frac{d_{t}}{2}} $$
- Normally we would use Thurstone's Law -- These guys didn't -- original data is not available to produce our own Thurstonian analysis -- we're stuck with IQRI - odd scale -- division by zero -- designed for unbalanced paired comparisons
